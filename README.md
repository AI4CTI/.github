# Welcome to AI4CTI 🛡️

![AI4CTI Logo](https://ai4cti.polito.it/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/ai4cti-b-d.webp)

**AI4CTI (Artificial Intelligence for Cyber Threat Intelligence)** is a high-impact research initiative focused on developing advanced AI-driven solutions to detect and mitigate social engineering and web-based threats in real-time.

---

### 🤝 Strategic Partnership
This project is a collaborative effort between academia and industry leaders:
*   **[Politecnico di Torino](https://polito.it)**: Leading research and scientific coordination via the Department of Control and Computer Engineering (DAUIN).
*   **[Ermes Browser Security](https://ermes.company)**: Providing industrial expertise and browser-based security technology.

*Funded by the Italian Fund for Applied Sciences (FISA).*

---

### 🚀 Our Mission
We aim to revolutionize web security by creating open-source tools and datasets that:
- 🔍 **Detect** sophisticated phishing and social engineering attacks.
- ⚡ **Automate** the analysis of malicious web content.
- 🛡️ **Protect** users across communication channels through AI-driven intelligence.
